ПРОГРАММА “Трансформация”

Transformation Program

“TRANSFORMASİYA” PROQRAMI

Эта программа создана на основе переосмысленного опыта изучения восточных философий на протяжении более чем 25 лет. Столько лет понадобилось, чтобы понять и отобрать самое практичное из того, что осталось нам от наших предков.

This program was created in the basis of a profound and rethought experience shaped by the study of Eastern philosophies for more than 25 years. It took many years to understand and select the most practical from our ancestors’ heritage.

Bu proqram 25 ildən artıq müddət ərzində şərq fəlsəfələrinin dərin araşdırmaları və təcrübələri əsasında yaradılmışdır. Ulularımızdan bizə qalan bilik və təcrübəni dərk edib və ən yararlı anlarını toplamaq üçün bu qədər zaman tələb olundu.

Transformation program delivered by Anar Mammadov is what is needed to return to peace be in charge of all your inner world and thoughts. When you accept this, you shine a bright light on your life. Transformation Program is also to provide a loving, supportive, nonjudgmental environment to nurture your spiritual growth. During the three modules in addition to the mediation techniques, energetic exercises and you also learn how to apply what you are learning in your daily life experience. There is post training support and peer group support available. Thus the program setting will help you uncover your own strength in a nurturing environment of safety and trust.

This environment makes it easy to release limiting concepts you no longer need. With the loving and never ending support of Anar Mammadov you learn to let down your defenses, your shields, even your denials as you realize you are not alone. As you do this, you can allow yourself to experience the joy and inner peace. There are many activities to help you connect feelings with ideas so you can make the ideas work in your life.
